Buttered Noodles

Description

You may be wondering: "Do I really need a recipe for buttered noodles?" The answer, of course, is yes. This kid-friendly recipe results in perfect noodles tossed in exactly the right amount of butter. Once you try these four-ingredient buttered noodles, you'll never go back to your old method again.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the fettuccine in lightly salted water until it's tender, yet still firm.
  2. Drain the pasta in a colander, then return it to the pot.
  3. Mix butter, cheese, salt, and pepper into the cooked noodles until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
